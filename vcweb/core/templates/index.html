Welcome

Welcome to the Virtual Commons Web Environment, an open software initiative hosted at Arizona State University. We develop real-time dynamic experiments to help us analyze and understand issues related to the governance of common resources. Sharing common resources has been a challenge for human societies for millennia. In many circumstances, the ability to create effective institutional arrangements has helped avoid the tragedy of the commons. Controlled experiments have been instrumental in increasing our understanding of how humans organize themselves in cooperative arrangements to govern common resources.

Current development

This application is still under active development and in its alpha stage. This means that any data you create on this website will not be preserved. Once we reach a public beta we will ensure that your data is maintained and migrated.

Current development is focused on the Lighter Footprints HTML 5 mobile application (also usable in the latest builds of Chrome and Safari). We are also planning to convert our field experiments on water, forests, and fisheries into web-based experiments, starting with the forestry experiment.

You can find up-to-date development news at our BitBucket repository.
