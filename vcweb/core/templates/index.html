Welcome

The Virtual Commons is an open software initiative hosted at Arizona State University. We develop real-time dynamic experiments to help us analyze and understand issues related to the governance of common resources. Sharing common resources has been a challenge for human societies for millennia. In many circumstances, the ability to create effective institutional arrangements has helped to avoid the tragedy of the commons. Controlled experiments have been instrumental in increasing our understanding of how people organize themselves in cooperative arrangements to govern common resources.

Current development

This website has been tested against up-to-date versions of Google Chrome, Mozilla Firefox and Safari. You can find up-to-date development news and documentation at our Bitbucket repository.
