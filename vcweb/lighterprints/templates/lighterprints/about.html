Welcome!

Our goal is to help you learn about and put into practice alternative activities and sustainable actions you can take to reduce your environmental footprint.

You can earn green points that are scaled to the relative impact of a particular alternative activity and unlock badges and more activities and actions by participating in the challenge.

If these activities are already a part of your daily life, good for you! You can still participate and compare your environmental footprint with your friends. If you'd like to get involved or have any comments or suggestions, please feel free to contact us.
