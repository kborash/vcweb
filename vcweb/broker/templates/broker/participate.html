How to play

You start this game with 10 hours available to invest. You will have several "workdays" which are the rounds of this game. At each workday you have to choose how you will allocate the time you have available between two activities. You can invest your time into harvesting a natural resource or into conservation/maintenance. Your gains are proportional to the amount of time you spend harvesting. For each hour you harvest you will receive $XX. The amount of time that you put on conservation will be sum together with the time that others invest in conservation. When conservation time is higher than 10 hours then your returns from the harvesting time will be 50% higher.

You can decide to invest all your time in one of the activities.
