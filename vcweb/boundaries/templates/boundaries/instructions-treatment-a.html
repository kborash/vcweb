Paid Experiment Instructions

Two Sessions

This experiment is composed of two different, unconnected sessions. Your group will be randomly selected for each session, so you will be playing with a different group each time. We will pay you for one of these two sessions, chosen at random. You have exactly 50% chance of being paid for your earnings in either session.

Group and Individual Status

You will be able to observe the status of the members of your group each round. Likewise your group members can see your status and those of the group.

Group Chat

You will be able to chat with the other members of your group via group text chat. Once you have made a harvest decision, you will no longer be able to chat for that round.

Harvest Choice

You will have a maximum of seconds to make a harvest decision each round. A countdown will alert you when there are seconds remaining to make a harvest choice. When the countdown reaches zero, any value you have selected will be submitted. The default harvest is zero.

Welcome to Session {{ session_number }}

In this session, there are two groups or villages. Each group has a forest that the other group cannot harvest. Both forests have the same growth capacity. In addition, you may observe the other group, their storage amounts, and their forest status. Similarly, they may observe your group in the same way.

Key points:

  • Two groups
  • Each group has an exclusive forest of the same size
  • You may observe the other group
  • They may observe your group
